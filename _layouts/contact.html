Getting There

Divine Retreat Centre, St Augustine’s Abbey is located on the tranquil seaside shores of Ramsgate in Kent, to the south-east of London. You can travel to the centre using various modes of transport.

  • Flight
    • The closest international airports to the Divine Retreat Centre are based in London (London Gatwick and London Heathrow).
    • For domestic or if flying from Europe, other London airports can also be used (London Luton is discouraged as it is the furthest away from the Divine Retreat Centre).

  • Train
    • There are frequent trains operated by Southeastern Railway from London terminals to Ramsgate. Tickets can be purchased online on https://www.southeasternrailway.co.uk or https://www.thetrainline.com/
    • The centre is a 15-20 minute walk from the station, or alternatively, you can take a taxi or bus (please alight at St. Augustine’s Abbey).

  • Local Bus in Ramsgate - Thanet Loop
    • The Thanet Loop is the most regular service which can take you from the railway station to the harbour side.
    • Other bus routes around Ramsgate include Number 9, Number 33/34, Number 34X, Number 38, Number 39, Number 42, Number 87/88.

  • By Car - From London
    • From London, the quickest route is via M2 with an average journey time of two hours.
